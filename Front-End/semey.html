Semey

Semey is a city in East Kazakhstan, known for its rich history
and culture, and is the birthplace of Abai and Shakarim.

Natural Attractions and Recreation

Ala–Kol Lake

A beautiful natural lake, perfect for relaxation, picnics, and outdoor activities like fishing and boating.

Bukhtarma Reservoir

A scenic water body ideal for water sports, fishing, and enjoying stunning landscapes.

City Life and Culture

Museums & Theaters

Visit the Abai Museum, Local History Museum, ans Semey Drama Theater for a taste of the city,s rich artistic heritage.

Cultural Events

Semey hosts cultural festivals like a Abai Day and Poetry evenings, celebrating music, literaure, ans Kazakh traditions.

Did you know

Semipalatinsk Suspension Bridge

"The longest suspension bridge in Central Asia"

Length: 1,086 meters

Location: Irtysh River

Year of opening: 2000

Japanese engineers participated in the project.

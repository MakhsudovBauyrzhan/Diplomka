Pavlodar

Pavlodar is a city in northeastern Kazakhstan, located on the banks of the Irtysh River.
Known for its industrial significance, rich history, and beautiful nature, the city blends culture and progress.

Natural Attractions and Recreation

Bayanaul National Park

A scenic escape just outside Pavlodar, known for its mountains, lakes, and hiking trails — ideal for camping and nature lovers.

Embankment of the Irtysh River

A peaceful riverside promenade perfect for walking, cycling, and enjoying beautiful sunsets over the Irtysh.

City Life and Culture

Museums & Theaters

Visit the Regional History Museum, Pavlodar Art Museum, and Chekhov Theater to explore the city's cultural life.

Cultural Events

Pavlodar hosts events like the Ertis Promenade concert series, local theater performances, and festivals celebrating regional traditions.

Did you know

Pavlodar’s Irtysh Bridge

"One of the most important transport links over the Irtysh River."

Length: 320 meters

Location: Spans the Irtysh River in Pavlodar

Year of opening: 2001

Supports railway and road transport, enhancing regional connectivity.

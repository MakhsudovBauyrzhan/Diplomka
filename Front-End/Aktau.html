Aktau

Aktau, located on the eastern shore of the Caspian Sea, is known for its stunning coastal views,
unique desert landscapes, and role as a major port and industrial center in western Kazakhstan.

Natural Attractions and Recreation

Caspian Sea Coast

Aktau’s beaches along the Caspian Sea offer opportunities for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying the sunsets over the water.

Bozzhyra Canyon

Located in the Ustyurt Plateau near Aktau, Bozzhyra features dramatic rock formations and breathtaking desert scenery.

City Life and Culture

City Center & Promenade

The Aktau waterfront promenade is a popular spot for walking, dining, and experiencing local culture with sea views.

Local Festivals

Aktau hosts cultural and music festivals during the summer, celebrating traditions and showcasing local and regional talent.

Did you know

Aktau’s Unique City Layout
